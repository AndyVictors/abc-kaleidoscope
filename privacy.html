Privacy Policy

for the ABC Kaleidoscope apps

This privacy policy applies to the ABC Kaleidoscope mobile apps offered through the Apple App Store. It is written for parents and children.

In short

Our apps collect no personal data. They run entirely on your device — no account, no tracking, no advertising, and no sharing of data with third parties.

2. What data we process

None. The apps do not collect, store, or transmit personal data. All content runs locally on the device. There is no sign-up, no analytics or statistics tools, and no crash reports sent to us.

3. Children

Our apps are designed for children. Because we do not collect any data, no personal data from children is processed. Consent under Article 8 GDPR is therefore not required.

4. Apple App Store

Downloads and any purchases are handled by the Apple App Store. Apple processes that data as an independent controller under Apple's own privacy policy. From Apple we receive only anonymous, aggregated sales statistics that cannot be traced to individuals.

5. No third parties

The apps include no third-party services — no analytics providers, no advertising networks, no social-media plugins.

6. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have rights including access, rectification, erasure, and objection. Because we hold no personal data about you, we generally have no data to which these rights could apply. For any questions, contact us at the email above. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

7. Changes to this policy

We will update this policy if our apps or the legal requirements change.
